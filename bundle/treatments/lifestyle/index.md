# Lifestyle Treatments

Behavioral, nutritional, and psychosocial interventions that form the foundation of diabetes self-management.

## Education and Nutrition

* [Diabetes Self-Management Education and Support (DSMES)](/treatments/lifestyle/dsmes.md) - Structured evidence-based education program delivered by a CDCES at four critical life/diabetes points to improve self-management, glycemia, and health outcomes.
* [Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT)](/treatments/lifestyle/medical-nutrition-therapy.md) - Individualized, RDN-delivered nutrition intervention addressing eating patterns, macronutrient quality, and practical dietary strategies.
* [Eating Patterns for Diabetes Management](/treatments/lifestyle/eating-patterns.md) - Overview of validated eating patterns, carbohydrate management approaches, and practical tools for dietary self-management.

## Weight and Activity

* [Weight Management in Diabetes](/treatments/lifestyle/weight-management.md) - Structured approach to weight loss using lifestyle, pharmacotherapy, and metabolic surgery, including targets, tools, and remission potential.
* [Physical Activity in Diabetes Management](/treatments/lifestyle/physical-activity.md) - Aerobic, resistance, and flexibility activity targets, including glycemic effects, sedentary behavior guidance, and precautions by complication status.

## Mental Health

* [Psychosocial Well-being and Mental Health](/treatments/lifestyle/psychosocial-wellbeing.md) - Mental health screening, diabetes distress, sleep health, and psychosocial barriers to care in people with diabetes.
