# Medications

Pharmacologic agents for glycemic management in Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes.

## Decision Framework

* [T2D Medication Algorithm](/treatments/medications/t2d-medication-algorithm.md) - Comorbidity-driven pharmacologic decision framework selecting agents based on ASCVD, heart failure, CKD, weight, and hypoglycemia risk rather than A1C alone.

## Oral Agents

* [Metformin](/treatments/medications/metformin.md) - First-line oral agent for T2D; mechanism, efficacy, eGFR-based dosing thresholds, GI management, and B12 monitoring requirements.
* [DPP-4 Inhibitors (Gliptins)](/treatments/medications/dpp4-inhibitors.md) - Oral agents that inhibit DPP-4, extending incretin action to reduce postprandial glucose without causing hypoglycemia or weight gain.

## Injectable and Cardiorenal Agents

* [GLP-1 Receptor Agonists](/treatments/medications/glp1-receptor-agonists.md) - GLP-1 RA pharmacology, clinical agents, MACE/weight/kidney benefits, GI tolerability, and tirzepatide in T2D management.
* [SGLT2 Inhibitors](/treatments/medications/sglt2-inhibitors.md) - SGLT2 inhibitor pharmacology, heart failure and CKD benefits, MACE data, euglycemic DKA risk in T1D, and sick-day rules.

## Insulin

* [Insulin Therapy in Type 1 Diabetes](/treatments/medications/insulin-therapy-t1d.md) - Insulin regimens for T1D including MDI, CSII, and AID systems; dose estimation, basal-prandial ratios, and automated insulin delivery.
* [Insulin Therapy in Type 2 Diabetes](/treatments/medications/insulin-therapy-t2d.md) - Indications, initiation strategies, and intensification algorithm for insulin in T2D, including combination with GLP-1 receptor agonists.
