# Treatments

Lifestyle and pharmacologic interventions for diabetes management.

## Lifestyle

* [Diabetes Self-Management Education and Support (DSMES)](/treatments/lifestyle/dsmes.md) - Structured evidence-based education program delivered by a CDCES at four critical life/diabetes points to improve self-management, glycemia, and health outcomes.
* [Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT)](/treatments/lifestyle/medical-nutrition-therapy.md) - Individualized, RDN-delivered nutrition intervention addressing eating patterns, macronutrient quality, and practical dietary strategies.
* [Eating Patterns for Diabetes Management](/treatments/lifestyle/eating-patterns.md) - Overview of validated eating patterns, carbohydrate management approaches, and practical tools for dietary self-management.
* [Weight Management in Diabetes](/treatments/lifestyle/weight-management.md) - Structured approach to weight loss using lifestyle, pharmacotherapy, and metabolic surgery, including targets, tools, and remission potential.
* [Physical Activity in Diabetes Management](/treatments/lifestyle/physical-activity.md) - Aerobic, resistance, and flexibility activity targets, including glycemic effects, sedentary behavior guidance, and precautions by complication status.
* [Psychosocial Well-being and Mental Health](/treatments/lifestyle/psychosocial-wellbeing.md) - Mental health screening, diabetes distress, sleep health, and psychosocial barriers to care in people with diabetes.

## Medications

* [T2D Medication Algorithm](/treatments/medications/t2d-medication-algorithm.md) - Comorbidity-driven pharmacologic decision framework selecting agents based on ASCVD, heart failure, CKD, weight, and hypoglycemia risk rather than A1C alone.
* [Metformin](/treatments/medications/metformin.md) - First-line oral agent for T2D; mechanism, efficacy, eGFR-based dosing thresholds, GI management, and B12 monitoring requirements.
* [GLP-1 Receptor Agonists](/treatments/medications/glp1-receptor-agonists.md) - GLP-1 RA pharmacology, clinical agents, MACE/weight/kidney benefits, GI tolerability, and tirzepatide in T2D management.
* [SGLT2 Inhibitors](/treatments/medications/sglt2-inhibitors.md) - SGLT2 inhibitor pharmacology, heart failure and CKD benefits, MACE data, euglycemic DKA risk in T1D, and sick-day rules.
* [DPP-4 Inhibitors (Gliptins)](/treatments/medications/dpp4-inhibitors.md) - Oral agents that inhibit DPP-4, extending incretin action to reduce postprandial glucose without causing hypoglycemia or weight gain.
* [Insulin Therapy in Type 1 Diabetes](/treatments/medications/insulin-therapy-t1d.md) - Insulin regimens for T1D including MDI, CSII, and AID systems; dose estimation, basal-prandial ratios, and automated insulin delivery.
* [Insulin Therapy in Type 2 Diabetes](/treatments/medications/insulin-therapy-t2d.md) - Indications, initiation strategies, and intensification algorithm for insulin in T2D, including combination with GLP-1 receptor agonists.
