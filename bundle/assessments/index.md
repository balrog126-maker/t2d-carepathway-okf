# Assessments

Evaluation frameworks and glycemic measurement tools used in ongoing diabetes care.

## Clinical Evaluation

* [Comprehensive Medical Evaluation](/assessments/comprehensive-medical-evaluation.md) - Structured initial and follow-up evaluation framework for people with diabetes covering history, physical exam, labs, psychosocial assessment, and care planning.

## Glycemic Measurement

* [A1C Assessment](/assessments/a1c-assessment.md) - A1C reflects average glycemia over ~3 months and serves as both a diagnostic criterion and glycemic management target, with important technical and patient-specific limitations.
* [CGM Metrics and Targets](/assessments/cgm-metrics.md) - Standardized continuous glucose monitoring metrics — TIR, TBR, TAR, GMI, and CV — with population-specific targets for interpreting glucose profiles.

## Goals and Risk

* [Glycemic Goals](/assessments/glycemic-goals.md) - Individualized targets for A1C, time in range, fasting, and postprandial glucose, balancing complication prevention against hypoglycemia risk.
* [Hypoglycemia Risk Assessment](/assessments/hypoglycemia-risk-assessment.md) - Identification of clinical and social risk factors for hypoglycemia, including impaired awareness and vulnerable populations, to guide prevention and medication selection.
