# T2D Care Pathway — OKF Bundle

Clinical knowledge graph for Type 2 Diabetes care pathways, structured per the
[Open Knowledge Format (OKF) v0.1](https://github.com/GoogleCloudPlatform/knowledge-catalog/blob/main/okf/SPEC.md).
Source: **ADA Standards of Care in Diabetes — 2026**.

## Conditions

* [Type 2 Diabetes](/conditions/type-2-diabetes.md) - Progressive non-autoimmune loss of β-cell insulin secretion on a background of insulin resistance; accounts for 90–95% of diabetes cases.
* [Type 1 Diabetes](/conditions/type-1-diabetes.md) - Autoimmune destruction of pancreatic β-cells leading to absolute insulin deficiency; staged from presymptomatic autoimmunity through overt hyperglycemia.
* [Prediabetes](/conditions/prediabetes.md) - Intermediate glucose/A1C state between normoglycemia and diabetes; carries significant cardiovascular and progression risk and warrants active intervention.
* [Diabetes Diagnosis Criteria](/conditions/diabetes-diagnosis-criteria.md) - Standardized plasma glucose and A1C thresholds for diagnosing diabetes and prediabetes; requires confirmatory testing except in unequivocal hyperglycemia.
* [Diabetes Screening](/conditions/diabetes-screening.md) - Risk-based approach to identifying prediabetes and T2D in asymptomatic adults and children, including targeted populations and recommended intervals.

## Comorbidities

* [Cardiovascular Disease and Risk Management](/conditions/comorbidities/cardiovascular-risk.md) - Comprehensive framework for ASCVD and heart failure prevention and treatment in diabetes, including blood pressure targets, lipid goals, and cardioprotective medications.
* [Chronic Kidney Disease and Diabetic Nephropathy](/conditions/comorbidities/ckd-nephropathy.md) - Progressive loss of kidney function in people with diabetes, assessed by eGFR and UACR, with implications for medication dosing and CV risk.
* [Autoimmune Comorbidities in Type 1 Diabetes](/conditions/comorbidities/autoimmune-comorbidities-t1d.md) - Common autoimmune conditions co-occurring with T1D — thyroid disease, celiac disease, and pernicious anemia — requiring periodic screening.
* [Bone Health and Fracture Risk](/conditions/comorbidities/bone-health-fracture-risk.md) - Diabetes increases fracture risk beyond what BMD alone predicts; assessment and medication selection must account for diabetes-specific skeletal fragility.

## Assessments

* [Comprehensive Medical Evaluation](/assessments/comprehensive-medical-evaluation.md) - Structured initial and follow-up evaluation framework covering history, physical exam, labs, psychosocial assessment, and care planning.
* [A1C Assessment](/assessments/a1c-assessment.md) - A1C reflects average glycemia over ~3 months and serves as both a diagnostic criterion and glycemic management target, with important technical and patient-specific limitations.
* [CGM Metrics and Targets](/assessments/cgm-metrics.md) - Standardized continuous glucose monitoring metrics — TIR, TBR, TAR, GMI, and CV — with population-specific targets for interpreting glucose profiles.
* [Glycemic Goals](/assessments/glycemic-goals.md) - Individualized targets for A1C, time in range, fasting, and postprandial glucose, balancing complication prevention against hypoglycemia risk.
* [Hypoglycemia Risk Assessment](/assessments/hypoglycemia-risk-assessment.md) - Identification of clinical and social risk factors for hypoglycemia, including impaired awareness and vulnerable populations, to guide prevention and medication selection.

## Monitoring

* [Glucose Monitoring Methods](/monitoring/glucose-monitoring.md) - Overview of BGM, CGM, and alternative biomarkers — indications, limitations, and selection by patient type.
* [Hypoglycemia Classification and Treatment](/monitoring/hypoglycemia-classification-treatment.md) - Three-level classification of hypoglycemia with glucose thresholds, acute treatment protocols, glucagon guidance, and prevention strategies.
* [Hyperglycemic Crises — DKA and HHS](/monitoring/hyperglycemic-crises.md) - DKA and HHS triggers, distinguishing features, prevention protocols, and sick-day management principles.

## Lifestyle Treatments

* [Diabetes Self-Management Education and Support (DSMES)](/treatments/lifestyle/dsmes.md) - Structured evidence-based education program delivered by a CDCES at four critical life/diabetes points to improve self-management and glycemia.
* [Medical Nutrition Therapy (MNT)](/treatments/lifestyle/medical-nutrition-therapy.md) - Individualized, RDN-delivered nutrition intervention addressing eating patterns, macronutrient quality, and practical dietary strategies.
* [Eating Patterns for Diabetes Management](/treatments/lifestyle/eating-patterns.md) - Overview of validated eating patterns, carbohydrate management approaches, and practical tools for dietary self-management.
* [Weight Management in Diabetes](/treatments/lifestyle/weight-management.md) - Structured approach to weight loss using lifestyle, pharmacotherapy, and metabolic surgery, including targets, tools, and remission potential.
* [Physical Activity in Diabetes Management](/treatments/lifestyle/physical-activity.md) - Aerobic, resistance, and flexibility activity targets, including glycemic effects, sedentary behavior guidance, and precautions by complication status.
* [Psychosocial Well-being and Mental Health](/treatments/lifestyle/psychosocial-wellbeing.md) - Mental health screening, diabetes distress, sleep health, and psychosocial barriers to care in people with diabetes.

## Medications

* [T2D Medication Algorithm](/treatments/medications/t2d-medication-algorithm.md) - Comorbidity-driven pharmacologic decision framework selecting agents based on ASCVD, heart failure, CKD, weight, and hypoglycemia risk rather than A1C alone.
* [Metformin](/treatments/medications/metformin.md) - First-line oral agent for T2D; mechanism, efficacy, eGFR-based dosing thresholds, GI management, and B12 monitoring requirements.
* [GLP-1 Receptor Agonists](/treatments/medications/glp1-receptor-agonists.md) - GLP-1 RA pharmacology, clinical agents, MACE/weight/kidney benefits, GI tolerability, and tirzepatide in T2D management.
* [SGLT2 Inhibitors](/treatments/medications/sglt2-inhibitors.md) - SGLT2 inhibitor pharmacology, heart failure and CKD benefits, MACE data, euglycemic DKA risk in T1D, and sick-day rules.
* [DPP-4 Inhibitors (Gliptins)](/treatments/medications/dpp4-inhibitors.md) - Oral agents that inhibit DPP-4, extending incretin action to reduce postprandial glucose without causing hypoglycemia or weight gain.
* [Insulin Therapy in Type 1 Diabetes](/treatments/medications/insulin-therapy-t1d.md) - Insulin regimens for T1D including MDI, CSII, and AID systems; dose estimation, basal-prandial ratios, and automated insulin delivery.
* [Insulin Therapy in Type 2 Diabetes](/treatments/medications/insulin-therapy-t2d.md) - Indications, initiation strategies, and intensification algorithm for insulin in T2D, including combination with GLP-1 receptor agonists.
