# Conditions

Diabetes types, diagnostic criteria, and screening — the foundational diagnostic layer of the T2D care pathway.

## Diabetes Types

* [Type 2 Diabetes](/conditions/type-2-diabetes.md) - Progressive non-autoimmune loss of β-cell insulin secretion on a background of insulin resistance; accounts for 90–95% of diabetes cases.
* [Type 1 Diabetes](/conditions/type-1-diabetes.md) - Autoimmune destruction of pancreatic β-cells leading to absolute insulin deficiency; staged from presymptomatic autoimmunity through overt hyperglycemia.
* [Prediabetes](/conditions/prediabetes.md) - Intermediate glucose/A1C state between normoglycemia and diabetes; carries significant cardiovascular and progression risk and warrants active intervention.

## Diagnosis and Screening

* [Diabetes Diagnosis Criteria](/conditions/diabetes-diagnosis-criteria.md) - Standardized plasma glucose and A1C thresholds for diagnosing diabetes and prediabetes; requires confirmatory testing except in unequivocal hyperglycemia.
* [Diabetes Screening](/conditions/diabetes-screening.md) - Risk-based approach to identifying prediabetes and T2D in asymptomatic adults and children, including targeted populations and recommended intervals.

## Comorbidities

* [Cardiovascular Disease and Risk Management](/conditions/comorbidities/cardiovascular-risk.md) - Comprehensive framework for ASCVD and heart failure prevention and treatment in diabetes.
* [Chronic Kidney Disease and Diabetic Nephropathy](/conditions/comorbidities/ckd-nephropathy.md) - Progressive loss of kidney function in people with diabetes assessed by eGFR and UACR.
* [Autoimmune Comorbidities in Type 1 Diabetes](/conditions/comorbidities/autoimmune-comorbidities-t1d.md) - Common autoimmune conditions co-occurring with T1D requiring periodic screening.
* [Bone Health and Fracture Risk](/conditions/comorbidities/bone-health-fracture-risk.md) - Diabetes-specific skeletal fragility and fracture risk beyond what BMD alone predicts.
