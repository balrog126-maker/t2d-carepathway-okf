# Comorbidities

Common conditions co-occurring with diabetes that influence treatment selection, monitoring frequency, and glycemic targets.

## Cardiovascular and Renal

* [Cardiovascular Disease and Risk Management](/conditions/comorbidities/cardiovascular-risk.md) - Comprehensive framework for ASCVD and heart failure prevention and treatment in diabetes, including blood pressure targets, lipid goals, antiplatelet therapy, and cardioprotective medications.
* [Chronic Kidney Disease and Diabetic Nephropathy](/conditions/comorbidities/ckd-nephropathy.md) - Progressive loss of kidney function in people with diabetes, assessed by eGFR and UACR, with implications for medication dosing and CV risk.

## Autoimmune and Metabolic

* [Autoimmune Comorbidities in Type 1 Diabetes](/conditions/comorbidities/autoimmune-comorbidities-t1d.md) - Common autoimmune conditions co-occurring with T1D — thyroid disease, celiac disease, and pernicious anemia — requiring periodic screening.
* [Bone Health and Fracture Risk](/conditions/comorbidities/bone-health-fracture-risk.md) - Diabetes increases fracture risk beyond what BMD alone predicts; assessment and medication selection must account for diabetes-specific skeletal fragility.
